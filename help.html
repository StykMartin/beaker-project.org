Getting further help

The best way to interact with Beaker developers and users is in the #beaker IRC channel on irc.freenode.net. The Beaker developers monitor this channel, and development discussions often happen there. Alternatively, you can post your question to the beaker-devel mailing list.

If you've found a bug in Beaker, please report it in Red Hat Bugzilla against the Beaker product.
